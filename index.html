I am a junior undergraduate student at Fuzhou University, majoring in Computer Science and Software Engineering (ranked 1st/89).

I am broadly interested in mechanistic interpretability of large language models (LLMs), particularly in understanding the internal mechanisms of LLMs and LLM-based agents. My research focuses on interpretability and visualization of model behaviors, including reasoning pathways, information flow, and geometric structures in latent representations. I was fortunate to intern at Shanghai AI Lab in 2025.
